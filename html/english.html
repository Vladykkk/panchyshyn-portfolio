Painting by Bogdan Panchyshyn

Works that have a deep philosophical meaning are characterized by an exquisite palette of colors and shades, a kind of aristocratic manner of presentation and subtle lyricism of images.

Biography

Bohdan Panchyshyn was born on October 17, 1958 in the village of Lubyana, Lviv region, Mykolaiv district. 1976 – to 1981 years. – studied at the Lviv Institute of Applied and Decorative Arts (today – Lviv Academy of Arts). Active creative life began in 1996 with a personal exhibition at the Lviv National Museum. Then several exhibitions in Odessa, Warsaw, Moscow, in the Presidential Palace of Bratislava, in Uzhgorod, Valencia, Nantes (Belgium). Participant of many art forums. In addition to oil, temper painting, is engaged in graphics
